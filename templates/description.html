HT-step Benchmark: Temporal Article Grounding.

Task

Temporal step grounding refers to finding the temporal segment in a video where a particular text query appears.

Dataset

HT-step contains instructional cooking videos that are temporally annotated with a sequence of steps taken from a WikiHow article. Each step consists of a headline sentence and a paragraph describing the step in more detail. Some steps of the article may not appear at all, appear partially, be executed in different order, or be repeated multiple times.
