HT100M-step: Temporal step grounding challenge

Task

Temporal step grounding refers to finding the temporal segment in a video where a particular text query appears. The task is therefore dense temporal grounding, as multiple textual queries (steps) are to be jointly grounded over the video.

Dataset

HTM100-step contains instructional cooking videos that are temporally annotated with a sequence of steps taken from a WikiHow article. Each step consists of a headline sentence and a paragraph describing the step in more detail. The steps of the article do not appear at all, appear partially, are executed in different order, or are repeated multiple times.
